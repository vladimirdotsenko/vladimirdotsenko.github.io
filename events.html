Events and some past talks

Events organised

  • I am one of the organisers of the closing conference of the ANR project "Higher Algebra, Geometry, and Topology"; it will take place in CIRM Luminy on May 6-10, 2024.
  • I organised (together with Evgeny Feigin and Markus Reineke) the mini-workshop "DT invariants of symmetric quivers: algebra, combinatorics, and topology"; it took place in Strasbourg on September 26-29, 2023.
  • I organised the mid-term workshop of the ANR project "Higher Algebra, Geometry, and Topology"; it took place in Strasbourg from May 30 to June 2, 2023.
  • I was a member of the scientific committee of the conference "Interactions and Applications of Homotopical Algebra and Geometry"; it took place at the University of Luxembourg on November 14-16, 2022.
  • I organised a working group on some categorical aspects of Noetherianity.
  • I organised (together with Frédéric Chapoton) the annual meeting of the French national research network of algebraic topology and its applications; it took place in Strasbourg on October 26-29, 2021.
  • I organised (together with Eduardo Marcos) the thematic section Homological methods" at XXIII Latin American Algebra Colloquium which took place in Mexico City on August 5-9, 2019.
  • I organised (together with Vincent Gélinas and Andrea Solotar) the workshop "Homotopy meets homology"; it took place in Dublin on May 27-31, 2019.
  • I was one of the local organisers of "The 14th William Rowan Hamilton Geometry and Topology Workshop" "Group actions and ergodic theory in geometry and topology"; it took place in Dublin on July 30-August 3, 2018.
  • I organised (together with Victoria Lebed) the workshop "Geometry and combinatorics of associativity"; it took place in Dublin on October 23-27, 2017.
  • I was one of the organisers of the third edition of the conference "Non-associative algebras and Lie theory"; it took place in Mexico City on February 27 - March 3, 2017.
  • I was one of the local organisers of "The 12th William Rowan Hamilton Geometry and Topology Workshop" "Low-dimensional topology, complex and symplectic geometry"; it took place in Dublin on August 22-26, 2016.
  • I was on the program committee of the workshop Higher-Dimensional Rewriting and Applications; it took take place in Oporto, Portugal, on June 25-26, 2016.
  • I was one of the local organisers of "The 11th William Rowan Hamilton Geometry and Topology Workshop" "Connections between three and four manifolds"; it took place in Dublin on August 25-29, 2015.
  • I was on the program committee of the workshop Higher-Dimensional Rewriting and Applications, to take place in Warsaw, Poland, on June 28, 2015.
  • I was one of the local organisers of "The 10th William Rowan Hamilton Geometry and Topology Workshop" "Homological invariants in low-dimensional topology and geometry"; it took place in Dublin on August 26-30, 2014.
  • I organised (together with Uli Kraehmer) a mini-workshop Batalin-Vilkovisky algebras, operads, and Hopf algebroids, it took place in Mathematisches Forschungsinstitut Oberwolfach on February 24-28, 2014.
  • I was one of the organisers of a conference dedicated to Borya Feigin on the occasion of his 60th anniversary, it took place in Moscow on December 13-19, 2013. Video recordings of talks at that conference are available here.
  • I was one of the local organisers of "The 9th William Rowan Hamilton Geometry and Topology Workshop" "Geometry and Groups after Thurston"; it took place in Dublin on August 27-31, 2013.
  • I organised (together with Norbert Poncin) a workshop "Higher algebras and Lie-infinity homotopy theory" at the University of Luxembourg, it took place on June 25-28, 2013.
  • In February 2013 I gave a mini-course "Operads, shuffles, and Gröbner bases" at the meeting Associative and Nonassociative Algebras and Dialgebras: Theory and Algorithms (in honour of Jean-Louis Loday), at CIMAT, Guanajuato, México.
  • In February 2012 I gave a mini-course "Rewriting methods for associative algebras and operads" at Algebra and computation, one of the workshops of the thematic programme "Logic and interactions 2012" held at CIRM Luminy, Marseille.
