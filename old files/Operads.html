Gröbner bases for operads

This webpage (in progress) contains various materials related to the implementation of Gröbner bases for operads, as defined by myself and Anton Khoroshkin in our paper (see also the arXiv version). This implementation was created by Willem Heijltjes under my guidance; it was supported by University of Luxembourg in 2012 and by Trinity College Dublin in 2014.
