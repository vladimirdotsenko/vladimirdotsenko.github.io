For visitors of University of Strasbourg

From the train station of Strasbourg (Gare Centrale), take tram C to the stop Université, walk down Rue Edmond Labbé from the stop, continue into Rue du Général Zimmer. After about 400m of walking in total (5 minutes), the mathematics institute building will be on your left. Take the elevator to the fifth floor.
